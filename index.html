Calculating cost

The cost of a cellphone plan is the amount paid initially and monthly fees over a 24 month period. Taxes, fees, etc. are assumed to be roughly the same, and left off.

For example, if you paid $300 for a phone, and your monthly bill was $30, we would perform the following calculation: 


								$300 + $30 * 24mo = $1,020 total cost

In this case our total cost for phone ownership is $1,020 in the 24 month period. To make plans easy to compare we'll divide by 24 months, to get the true per month cost. It's important to do this as there are other costs that factor in. 


								$1,020 / 24 = $42.50mo

Common factors

  • All scenarios assume an iPhone 6 valued at $650
  • All scenarios assume a two year contract period
  • All scenarios require a minimum of 2gb of data a month

The big three

ATT

  • $200 one time phone
  • $40 monthly smartphone access monthly
  • $40 monthly service

ATT true monthly


								($200 + ($40 + $40 * 24)) / 24 = $88.33mo

ATT iPhone6 true cost


								$200 + ($40 * 24) = $1,160

Sprint

  • $200 one time phone
  • $45 monthly smartphone access monthly
  • $40 monthly service

Sprint true monthly


								($200 + ($45 + $40 * 24)) / 24 = $93.33mo

Sprint iPhone6 true cost


								$200 + ($45 * 24) = $1,280

Verizon

  • $200 one time phone
  • $40 monthly smartphone access monthly
  • $30 monthly service

Verizon true monthly


								($200 + ($40 + $30 * 24)) / 24 = $78.33mo

Verizon iPhone6 true cost


								$200 + ($40 * 24) = $1,160

Minimize

There are two steps to paying less for your cellphone bill monthly. The first step is to find a MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator), and the second step is to bring a compatible device.

Cricket wireless - ATT MVNO

A monthly plan will run you $40, but you also have to pay full price for the iPhone 6. 


								($650 + ($40 * 24)) / 24 = $67
							


								$88.33 (ATT) - $67 (Cricket) = 21.33mo
							


								21.33mo * 24mo = $511.92 saved

Boost Mobile - Sprint MVNO

A monthly plan will run you $45, but you also have to pay fill price for the iPhone 6. 


								($650 + ($45 * 24)) / 24 = $72.08
							


								$93.33 (Sprint) - $72.08 (Boost Mobile) = 21.25mo
							


								21.25mo * 24 = $510 saved

PagePlus - Verizon MVNO

A monthly plan will run you $55, but you also have to pay fill price for the iPhone 6. 


								($650 + ($55 * 24)) / 24 = $82.08
							


								$78.33mo (Verizon) - $82.08 (PagePlus) = $ -3.75mo
							


								$ -3.75mo * 24 = $ -90 saved

Another way?

What happens when we swap in a $200 unlocked smartphone? We'll assume the big three give us the phone for FREE!

ATT/Cricket


								$80 * 24 (ATT) - $200 + $40 * 24 (Cricket) = $760 saved

Sprint/Boost Mobile


								$85 * 24 (Sprint) - $200 + $45 * 24 (Cricket) = $760 saved

Verizon/PagePlus


								$75 * 24 (Verizon) - $200 + $45 * 24 (Cricket) = $280 saved
